SALES SCRIPT
Door-to-door · Merchant Processing · Fairfield County

OPENER — WALK IN COLD

First words:
"Hey how's it going — is the owner or manager around for just 2 minutes? I'm not here to sell you anything today, just want to introduce myself real quick."
If they ask what it's about:
"Credit card processing — I work with a lot of shops in Fairfield County and most of them are saving a few hundred bucks a month just by switching. I literally just want to leave my card and take a quick look at a statement if you've got one."

DISCOVERY — OWNER IS THERE

"How long have you been taking cards here? … And are you on a flat rate or interchange-plus right now?"
"What processor are you with? … Do you know your effective rate — the total percentage you're actually paying across all card types?"
"If I could cut that by 20-30%, no contract, same-day deposits — would that be worth 10 minutes while I'm here?"

STATEMENT REVIEW

"Can you grab your most recent processing statement? Even just one month — I'll do a free analysis right here and show you exactly what you're paying versus what you should be paying."
"Most owners think they're on a good rate until I break it down. Effective rate is usually 3-4% all in. I get most businesses down to 1.8-2.5%."

OBJECTION HANDLERS

❌ "Happy with my current processor"
"That's what they all say — until they see their actual rate broken down. Let me do a free comparison right now, takes 5 minutes. If yours is truly the best deal I'll tell you straight up."
❌ "I signed a contract"
"When does it expire? … Perfect — let's build the comparison now so when it's up you're ready to move immediately. I'll come back when you're free."
❌ "Too busy right now"
"Totally get it — I'm in this area Tuesdays and Thursdays. Can I grab 10 minutes then? I'll bring coffee."
❌ "Need to talk to my partner/accountant"
"Of course — when are you both here? I'll bring the analysis so they can see the numbers side by side. What day works?"
❌ "I've tried switching before and it was a nightmare"
"I hear that a lot — and honestly most processors make it complicated on purpose. I handle the whole transition. You literally don't touch anything. Same equipment, same day deposits, lower rate."

THE CLOSE

"Based on what I'm seeing, you'd save roughly $[X] per month. Setup is free, no contract. The only question is — is there any reason not to start saving money this week?"
"Let's do this right now. I need a voided check and your last 3 statements. I can have you live in 48 hours. Want to get it done while I'm here?"

FUNERAL HOME — SPECIFIC OPENER

"Hi, I work with funeral homes across Fairfield County on their payment processing. Families paying during the hardest time of their lives — we make sure the transaction side is seamless, and that you're not overpaying on every swipe. Five minutes for the director?"
